To cast a vote, you will be led through the following steps:

  1. Select your options.
    Answer the questions, and review your choices.
    You are anonymous at this point (login is at step 3, after encryption).

  2. Encrypt your selection.
    Your selection is encrypted safely inside in your browser.

  3. Submit your encrypted ballot.
    Record your ballot tracking number.
    Proceed to log in and record your encrypted ballot for tallying.
    (Instead of submitting, you can opt to audit your ballot to verify the encryption.)
